Common questions
Before you come
Do I need to know how to code? +
Not at all. Designers, product people, students, and people who are just curious about tech are a regular part of our crowd. You'll have plenty to talk about.
What language are events in? +
Both English and Japanese. Most conversations are bilingual and we try to make sure nobody feels left out. If you speak one language, you'll be fine.
Is it okay to come alone? +
That's how most people come the first time. We'll introduce you around — it's part of what we do.
Is there a membership fee? +
No membership. Most events are free. Occasionally a venue or workshop has a small fee — it's always listed clearly on the event page.
I'm not fluent in Japanese. Will I be lost? +
Not at all. A good chunk of our community are expats or internationals based in Kyoto, and events naturally mix languages. You'll find plenty of English conversations.
How do I stay in the loop between events? +
Join our Discord — that's where most between-event conversation happens. We also post updates on Meetup and occasionally on the community feed here.
